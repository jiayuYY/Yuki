My name is Yuki (Jiayu) Yin. I am an PhD student at UCSC Computational Media study Human-Computer Interaction, advised by Prof. David Lee. My research focus on Human-AI collboration, cognitive scaffolding, and interactive systems. I design and build interfaces that empower human creativity and learning.
Before my PhD study, I gained my M.S degree at UC Irvine Informatics advised by Prof. Madhu Reddy and worked at the UCSD Design Lab advised by Prof. Steven Dow and Prof. Jane E. I gained my B.A degree at Tsinghua University, where I worked with Prof. Pei-Luen Patrick Rau.
